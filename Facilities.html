Modern Classrooms

Our classrooms are equipped with the latest audio-visual technology to facilitate dynamic and interactive learning. From large lecture halls to smaller seminar rooms, each space is designed to encourage collaboration and engagement.

Library Resources

Access a wealth of computer science literature, journals, and reference materials in our dedicated library section. The library is equipped with online resources to support both coursework and research, ensuring that students have comprehensive access to the latest developments in the field.

Facilities at University of Ghana Computer Science

Collaborative Spaces

Foster creativity and teamwork in our collaborative spaces, designed for group projects, study sessions, and informal discussions. These areas provide a dynamic environment for students to exchange ideas and work together on projects.

Auditorium and Event Spaces

Participate in or host events, conferences, and seminars in our auditorium and event spaces. These venues are equipped with modern audio-visual systems, making them ideal for presentations, workshops, and gatherings that foster a sense of community within the department.

Student Lounges

Relax and connect with fellow students in our dedicated lounges. These spaces are designed for socializing, networking, and building a sense of camaraderie among the computer science community.
