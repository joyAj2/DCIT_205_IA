Computer Science Department Curriculum

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

An overview of fundamental concepts in computer science, including programming languages, algorithms, and data structures. A foundation in mathematical principles essential for understanding and solving computational problems. Computer Systems and Architecture Exploring the architecture of computers and the fundamentals of computer systems. Second Year

Data Structures and Algorithms

In-depth study of data structures and algorithms, emphasizing problem-solving and efficiency. Principles and practices of software development, including version control, testing, and collaborative coding. Database Management Systems Introduction to database design, implementation, and management. Third Year

Operating Systems

Understanding the core concepts and functionalities of operating systems. Exploration of network protocols, security principles, and techniques to safeguard digital assets. Artificial Intelligence Introduction to AI concepts, machine learning algorithms, and applications.
